Privacy Policy

Keys Trip 2026 — Last updated: May 2026

Overview

Keys Trip 2026 is a private group vacation coordination app used exclusively by a pre-registered group of 8 participants traveling together in July 2026. This policy describes how we collect and use information.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to operate the app:

  • Name (selected from a pre-defined list of 8 participants)
  • Location check-ins (entered manually by the user)
  • Photos uploaded voluntarily by participants
  • Mobile phone numbers (provided by the trip organizer for SMS notifications)

SMS Notifications

Participants receive SMS notifications when other group members check in to a location. These messages are sent only to the 8 pre-registered participants. Message frequency varies based on group activity. Message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP to unsubscribe. Reply HELP for help.

How We Use Information

Information is used solely to coordinate the group vacation. No data is sold, shared with third parties, or used for marketing purposes.

Data Storage

Check-ins and photos are stored in Firebase (Google) and are accessible only to registered participants. Data is deleted after the trip.
